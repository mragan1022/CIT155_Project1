Are "Jones" handlebars right for you?

Why to use Jones handlebars:

The Jones Loop H-Bar sweeps forward to allow the levers to go behind the cross bar instead of in front. This gives longer uninterrupted hand positions and will allow trigger and grip/twist shifters to fit on the rearward extensions. The continuous front loop of the bar enhances stiffness and is good for additional aero hand positions and mounting lights, GPS devices etc. With the levers in the optimum position, you get a wide range of hand and body positions, from upright at the ends of the grips, to a tucked aero position when resting your forearms across the crossbar and loop.

Try wrapping the crossbar with bar-tape! You can wrap both the crossbar and loop, if you want to have the most hand positions! Combined with the bar-tape with the comfortable Jones Kraton H-Grips, your hands will be happy!
