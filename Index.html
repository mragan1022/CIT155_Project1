What are the right bicycle handlebars for you?

The purpose of the handlebars is threefold; provide a mechanism to steer the bicycle, second provide balance and stability as you ride, and third provide a comfortable place for your hands.

The first two purposes are straightforward and most handlebars address these needs, however the need for comfort is more difficult to achieve.

Many people will use the handlebars that came stock on their bicycles and never give a second thought to changing them. As an individual spends more time and duration on their bicycle, comfort becomes an important factor. Handlebar comfort can fall into; primary hand position, number of alternative hand positions, grips, tape, padding, weight distribution, and body position.

Below are a few examples of handlebars you might want to try!

  • Drop Bars
  • Straight Bars
  • Butterfly Bars
  • Jones Bars
